Hi! I'm Johann, a British-New Zealand Computer Science student based in the UK, set to achieve a Computer Science MSci from the University of St Andrews in 2028.


My main interests in programming tend to be in low-level systems and Operating Systems in particular. I also have interests in the design, uses, and implementations of programming languages and I have conducted my own experimentation in many-valued logic computers.


I'm not just a computer nerd, I also enjoy:

  • Music - both listening to it (which I almost always am) and playing one of my guitars or basses.
  • Languages - owing to my heritage, I speak German and have a basis in Classical Latin; I'd love to learn many more, in particular Hungarian and Māori.
  • Travelling - I hope to get a remote job someday and with that I'd love to work and travel around the world.
  • Nature - long walks are really calming and offer a much needed break from debugging.
  • Video Games - in particular Stellaris, KOTOR, and SuperHot.
