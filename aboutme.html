I'm Johann, a British-New Zealand Computer Science student based in the UK, set to achieve a Computer Science MSci from the University of St Andrews in 2028.


My main interests in programming tend to be in low-level systems and Operating Systems in particular. I also have interests in the design, uses, and implementations of programming languages and I have conducted my own experimentation in many-valued logic computers. I use arch btw.

I'm the lead developer of Kumomi Systems, which I founded in 2023.

  • Travelling - I hope to get a remote job someday and with that I'd love to work and travel around the world.
