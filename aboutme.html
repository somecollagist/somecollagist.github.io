I'm Johann, a British-New Zealand Computer Science student based in the UK, set to achieve a Computer Science MSci from the University of St Andrews in 2028.


My main interests are in Low-level Programming and Operating Systems in particular. I also have interests in the design, uses, and implementations of programming languages and I have conducted my own experimentation in ternary and quinary computing. I use Arch btw.

I'm the lead developer of Kumomi Systems, which I founded in 2023, and a member of The AVISI Group.

  • Music - both listening to it (which I almost always am) and playing guitar, bass, or ukulele.
  • Languages - owing to my heritage, I speak German and have a basis in Classical Latin; I'd love to learn many more, in particular Hungarian and Māori.
  • Travelling - I hope to get a remote job someday and with that I'd love to work and travel around the world.
  • Sporting - long walks are calming and help me to debug, and I also enjoy going to the gym and practising Aikido.
  • Games - in particular Stellaris, Dungeons and Dragons, and Cribbage.
